Difference Between Article, Section and Div tag

Sr. No. Section-Tag Div-Tag Article tag
1. The "section" element represents a generic section of a document or application. The "div" element has no special meaning at all. The "article" element represents a independent item section of content. It must have a "h1 tag for title.
2. A section, in this context, is a thematic grouping of content:
— chapter
— various tabbed pages in a tabbed dialog box
— numbered sections of a thesis 		It can be used with the class, lang, and title attributes to mark up semantics common to a group of consecutive elements. Independent item section of content:
— forum post
— magazine article
— newspaper article
— blog entry
— user-submitted comment
3. A Web site’s home page could be split into sections for an introduction, news items, and contact information.
